It was a dark and stormy night...

The winds were howling...

Suddenly, a scream was heard

You turn around and walk in the direction of the scream to investigate

You stop at a road, then you see a car crashed into a tree

Upon moving closer you notice something strange lying in the drivers seat. It appears to be a severed arm, and the seat is covered in blood.

You follow a trail of blood that leads you to an old cabin, the door is wide open, and there is a dim light inside.

Inside you find a man sitting in a chair holding an axe, he smiles at you, you go to run but the door shuts behind you.
